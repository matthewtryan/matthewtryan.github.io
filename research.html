Research

I am an undergraduate researcher in the Plant and Insect Ecology department at Penn State. I work in 314 Mueller Building in the Stephenson Laboratory under Dr. Stephenson and Ph.D student Alex Serpi.

Currently, I am focusing on the interrelationships among inbreeding, herbivory, and the transmission of bacterial and viral diseases vectored by the tobacco hornworms in the lab. Under the same subject, the main question I am seeking to answer is how does inbreeding affect the rates of exposure to pathogens transmitted by herbivores?

Some of the laboratory techniques I am familiar with are GFP labeling of bacterial pathogens, microarrays, PCR, and DAS-ELISA immunoassays to identify and track pathogens.

If/when I have published a paper regarding this research, I will post it here.
